摘要 / Abstract

麦克风阵列波束形成技术已广泛应用于各种不同的实际场景中。为了有效地抑制噪声同时高保真地拾取宽带期望语音信号，所设计的波束形成器需要有较高的空间增益，较好的频率一致性以及较强的稳健性。为此，学术界已提出了大量算法，其中，最近提出的基于线性阵列和矩形阵列的克罗内克积波束形成方法展现出了诸多优势。本文将该方法扩展到三维阵列。针对三维立方阵列，本文提出将全局波束形成滤波器分解为两个子滤波器的克罗内克积，并通过独立地设计子滤波器使全局滤波器能获取较高的指向性因子同时能在三维空间中自由导向。

Microphone array beamforming has been widely used in a wide range of acoustic applications. To make it effective in suppressing noise, yet being able to preserve the fidelity and quality of broadband speech signals of interest, the beamformer needs to be designed with high spatial gain, consistent responses at different frequencies, and high robustness against array imperfections. A great deal of efforts have been devoted in the literature to achieving this goal, among which the Kronecker product beamforming method, developed recently for linear and rectangular microphone arrays, has demonstrated some interesting properties. In this work, we extend this approach to three-dimensional arrays. Focusing on cuboid shape of microphone arrays, we first decompose the global beamforming filter into a Kronecker product of two sub-beamforming filters. Algorithms are then developed to design each sub-beamforming filter so that the global beamformer has a high directivity factor and can be steered flexibly in the three-dimensional space.
